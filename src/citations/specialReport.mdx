IPCC estimates that humans have already raised global temperates by 1.0ºC above pre-industrial levels. At our current rate, they estimate we will reach 1.5ºC by 2030, which is the point of no return.

Source: [IPCC, 2018: Summary for Policymakers. In: Global Warming of 1.5°C, A.1](https://www.ipcc.ch/sr15/chapter/spm/#article-spm-a)
