In the last 50 years the number of humans on this planet has doubled. There were approximately **1** billion people in 1800 and now we approach **8**.

| Year | World Population | Increase                  |
| ---- | ---------------- | ------------------------- |
| 1800 | 1 000 000 000    | 1 B in 200 000 years      |
| 1900 | 1 600 000 000    | 0.6 B in 100 years (+60%) |
| 1950 | 2 536 431 000    | 0.94 B in 50 years (+59%) |
| 1970 | 3 700 437 000    | 1.16 B in 20 years (+46%) |
| 1990 | 5 327 231 000    | 1.63 B in 20 years (+44%) |
| 2000 | 6 143 494 000    | 0.82 B in 10 years (+15%) |
| 2010 | 6 956 823 000    | 0.81 B in 10 years (+13%) |
| 2020 | 7 794 799 000    | 0.84 B in 10 years (+12%) |

Source: [UN DESA / Population Division, 2019: World Population Prospects 2019](https://population.un.org/wpp/Download/Standard/Population/)
