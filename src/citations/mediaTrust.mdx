2016 marked an all-time low with only 32% of Americans trusting in Mass Media "a great deal" or "a fair amount".

2018 showed an increased to 45%, but over half of Americans still report that they do not trust mainstream media and that number is even lower with youth (38%).

Source: [Gallup, 2018: U.S. Media Trust Continues to Recover From 2016 Low](https://news.gallup.com/poll/243665/media-trust-continues-recover-2016-low.aspx)
